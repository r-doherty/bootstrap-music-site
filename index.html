ABOUT

Made in KC opened in May of 2015 to provide local Kansas City artists and makers a platform to reach customers in the greater metro area.

We curate our products from hundreds of submissions and work to support brands through partnerships, relationships, and resources. The artists we work with make or design their products in the Kansas City metro area and may source some parts of their products outside Kansas City when a non-local option is not available or extremely cost prohibitive. We work actively to build our network of vendors and resources so that we can help brands grow their sales but also build a more sustainable business.
